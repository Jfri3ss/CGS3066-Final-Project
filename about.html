About Us

With constant indulging code dishes coming out of our kitchens, many patrons may not know (or care) about who we are and what strides we took to make Table 3066 the restaurant it is today. Table 3066 is a nod to the past code lovers of FSU’s CCI program and anybody else with a love for great code. Starting back in 2022, our three founders Kelly, Jeremiah, and Lauren bonded together over their love for good bites and an even greater love for bytes. All from seemingly different backgrounds, the 3 chefs transformed this love for computers and cuisine into a profitable business. Inspired by the stories of FSU’s hardworking students, Table 3066 embraces a space where people come together and enjoy homemade code and so much more with their peers, family, and friends alike.
